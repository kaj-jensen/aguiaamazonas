## Editorial rules

- Primary tour images must be sharp, inviting and geographically credible.
- Documentary snapshots may support an itinerary, but must not be used as the principal sales image unless they meet the same visual standard.
- Wildlife imagery must not imply a guaranteed sighting.
- Stock imagery must illustrate an activity genuinely offered by the corresponding tour.
- Keep the photographer, source page and license recorded here even when attribution is not mandatory.
